## The Housing Crisis

### The Problem
Students relocating to new cities face dangerous roommate mismatches and unsafe housing situations that compromise their safety, mental wellbeing, and academic performance.

### Who's Affected
- University students moving to major cities  
- International students seeking local connections  
- First-time renters with limited experience  

Poor roommate matching doesn't just affect living comfort‚Äîit impacts academic success, mental health, and personal safety in profound ways.

## Our AI-Powered Solution

### 01 Profile Reader Agent
Intelligently extracts and categorizes key lifestyle attributes from unstructured housing advertisements and user profiles

### 02 Match Scorer Agent
Computes deep compatibility scores using advanced algorithms that consider lifestyle, preferences, and behavioral patterns

### 03 Red Flag Detector
Proactively identifies potential conflicts and safety concerns before they become problems in shared living situations

### 04 Wingman Advisor
Provides transparent explanations for matches and suggests practical compromises to bridge compatibility gaps
